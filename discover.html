Discover: UMD Duluth & Duluth

Why I chose UMD Duluth

I came to UMD because I'd heard it's such a nice place with lots of attractions, and because it has a new business school and strong finance programs. My brother went here and several of my friends also attended, so I already felt connected to the campus before I arrived.

What I like about Duluth

What I like most about Duluth is being close to my friends and having the free time to enjoy the area. I love Lake Superior and Canal Park — they are great places to relax and explore. I also use the campus gym a lot. Beyond those, Duluth has nice local businesses, outdoor trails, and a friendly community.
