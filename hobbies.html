Sports

Summer is my favorite time to get outside and enjoy my favorite hobbies. I like spending my time with friends and family at the lake. Whether it's water activities or fishing, no matter what it will be fun. The best part of summer is the freedom and golf.

Photography

I enjoy capturing landscapes and candid moments with my camera. Photography helps me notice details and practice patience — I often take photos during trips around Duluth and at the lake.
